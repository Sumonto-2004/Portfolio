About Me

As a dedicated student pursuing a BSc in Material Science and Engineering at BUET, I am passionate about exploring the properties and applications of materials to solve real-world problems. My academic journey has equipped me with a strong foundation in materials characterization, nanotechnology, and sustainable materials development.

I have actively engaged in various research projects, including sodium-based batteries and advanced composite materials. These experiences honed my analytical skills and deepened my appreciation for innovative materials science solutions.

I strive to be a kind, knowledgeable person who helps others thrive in a happier world.
